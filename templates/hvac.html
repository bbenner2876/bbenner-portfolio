Living Building: HVAC Control App

Mobile application created to facilitate fair use and joint control over HVAC units in the new Living Buildings at UCSD.

Project Outline

New buildings are being created at UCSD which will seek to reduce energy usage by limiting HVAC (Heating Ventilation Air Conditioning) to zones of offices. Each zone will consist of 4 offices with 1 individual in each office primarily. The client has asked for an app or website that will facilitate the fair and efficient use of the HVAC controls within a zone.

Goals

  • Allow users to work in a comfortable temperature
  • Allow users to fairly and judiciously agree on settings for HVAC
  • Allow users to remain in control of their office space
  • Allow users to communicate with fellow individuals in their same HVAC space
  • Reduce overall HVAC usage

Constraints

  • HVAC control are located in one room in a zone
  • HVAC will shut off if any window is open
  • HVAC controls can only be accessed physically

Assumptions

  • Users will have different preferences in temperature
  • Users will occasionally want to open their windows
  • Users will be willing to negotiate and communicate
