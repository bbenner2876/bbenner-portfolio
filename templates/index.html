About

Welcome to my site! I originally hail from San Diego where I developed a love for alt-rock, carne asada, and sun burns. Outside of UX design I am keeping myself busy reading every Murakami book, reducing my closet to black or white t-shirts, and eating at Chan Chan Food House!
