About

Welcome to my site! I originally hail from San Diego where I developed a love for alt-rock, carne asada, and sun burns. Just recently I graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction. I am currently working in Orange County as a UX Designer & Researcher.


My goals for the quarter are to continue developing my mock-up skills with Figma, study best design practices online, and keep my JavaScript knowledge up to par with JavaScript and jQuery: Interactive Front-End Web Development.


Outside of UX design I am keeping myself busy reading every Murakami book, reducing my closet to black or white t-shirts, and eating at Chan Chan Food House!
