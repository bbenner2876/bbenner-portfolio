UCSD: Parking App Redesign

Redesigning the UCSD Parking App from scratch with a new focus on data visualization and more control for users.

The Outline

Background of The UCSD App

  • Primarily used by students, staff, and faculty at UCSD
  • Occasionally visitors
  • Consists of unique cards and section
  • Project focuses on the parking card/section

First design

  • User subscribes to 1-3 parking lots or is given default parking lots
  • Percentage availability of parking is displayed for these lots and sneak peeks at availability of other lots
  • Customizable by parking permit

Feedback

  • Utilize the system of lots as a whole
  • Display trends or data over time
  • Utilize lesser used lots that users might not be aware of
  • More flexibility over all

Re-design

  • Interactive map of parking lots
  • Allows for navigation through gesturing or searching
  • Users can see the number of spots of nearby parking locations within an area
  • Users can select which permits and parking spots to use
  • Selecting a lot will give more detailed information + trends in parking
  • Useful for estimating how parking will change by the time the user arrives
  • Users can search for locations or lots
  • Locations will pop up on the map
  • Users can then select nearby lots to see the distance
  • Shown as the dotted red line
  • Users can choose to navigate to selected locations or to lots through the app which would then open Google Maps directly
